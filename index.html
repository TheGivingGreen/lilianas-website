Welcome to the Family

Food Made Like We're Cooking for You at Home

Liliana's Italian Kitchen is South County's go-to spot for homestyle Italian — the kind that tastes like someone's nonna made it. We're a family restaurant in every sense: family owned, family operated, and named after our daughter, Liliana.

Tim Pieri brings 30 years of restaurant experience to every plate — house-roasted meats, fresh-made pasta, award-winning St. Louis-style pizza, and Italian sandwiches served in portions big enough to mean it.
