From hidden gem to household name. Here's the coverage that got us there.

64 competitors, 6 rounds of elimination. Liliana's claimed the title for the second consecutive year — the only restaurant to win back-to-back in the bracket's history.

St. Louis Magazine · KMOV Channel 4
A-List Winner + Great Day St. Louis Feature
Named Hidden Gem Restaurant of the Year by St. Louis Magazine's A-List Awards. Also recognized as finalist for St. Louis-Style Pizza and Top 5 Italian Restaurant. Food critic George Mahe featured the Garlic Shrimp Pizza on KMOV's Great Day St. Louis.

St. Louis Magazine

Named one of the top new restaurant openings of the year by St. Louis Magazine. Recognized for fresh ingredients, generous portions, and a neighborhood feel that South County had been waiting for.

Riverfront Times
Old-School Italian Includes the City's Best St. Louis-Style Pizza
RFT's deep dive into Tim Pieri's pizza obsession — from Rossino's to Liliana's — calling the crust one of the finest examples of St. Louis-style pizza in the city.
